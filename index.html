Privacy First

Your guests' names never leave your building

Before anything is sent to an AI model, Tidemark replaces every piece of sensitive information with anonymous codes — right on your computer. The AI only ever sees codes like [GUEST_1] and [ROOM_2].

1
Detected locally Names, room numbers, staff IDs, and phone numbers are found on your device before anything happens.
2
Replaced with codes Real values are swapped for anonymous placeholders. This happens in memory — never written to disk.
3
Restored after processing Once the AI returns the polished report, real names are restored locally. The codes are then discarded.
